# Reading Advantage
## Teacher Training Guide

**Complete Teacher-Led Lesson Plan**

Secondary EFL/ESL Students (CEFR A1–C1)
Duration: 90-100 minutes

---

# Core Instructional Goal

This lesson teaches students how to **use the Reading Advantage app effectively on their own** by **modeling every action** in a whole-class environment.

**At Every Step, Teachers:**
- **Explain** what students are doing
- **Explain** why they are doing it
- **Model** how to do it well

---

# Before Class Preparation

1. Open Reading Advantage and load the target article
2. Confirm audio plays clearly and sentence highlighting works
3. Prepare student workbooks and pens/pencils
4. Decide which steps may be shortened if time runs low

# Final Message

> **"This is exactly how you should use Reading Advantage by yourself."**

---

# Key Takeaways

✓ **Model every action** — students learn by watching first
✓ **Explain the WHY** — not just the what
✓ **Maintain consistent routines** — reduces cognitive load
✓ **Check for completion** — every student, every step
✓ **Evidence-based reading** — always refer back to the text
